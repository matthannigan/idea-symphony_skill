## Notable Deviations

1. **Mobile-app convergence at 12:** Both q08-12 and q10-15 produced exactly 12 questions for mobile-app. This suggests the Analyst perceives a natural saturation point for this topic around 12 questions, likely because the mobile-app topic is already analytically framed (metrics-driven, feature-prioritization focused) so fewer dimensions need opening up. This is not a problem — it confirms the Analyst exercises judgment about natural coverage rather than mechanically filling to the target ceiling.

2. **Youth-mentorship slight overshoot (16):** The only topic to exceed the q10-15 range ceiling. Youth mentorship has many analytical dimensions (risk, economics, matching algorithms, outcome measurement, systems integration) that each warrant decomposition. The overshoot is minor and the extra question is defensible, though it does create slight redundancy.

3. **Space-party meta-analysis at q10-15:** The addition of a "Frameworks & Reusable Systems" cluster at the 15-question level is an interesting behavior. The Analyst, when given volume headroom on a lightweight topic, shifts to meta-level analytical questions about party-planning methodology rather than generating more party-specific questions. This is characteristic of the analytical perspective — systematizing even casual activities — but the user did not ask for reusable planning frameworks.

4. **Question length slightly longer than V1 take 2 suggested:** V1 take 2 noted that questions at volume 5 were "extremely long and multi-layered" but became "properly bounded" at 5-10. At q08-12 and q10-15, questions are single-dimension but still tend to be longer than other personas — many include contextual framing, sub-questions for clarification, and explicit decomposition language ("breaking this down..."). This is not filler but rather the Analyst's characteristic style. It was present in V1 take 2 as well and is not a deviation.

**No finding from V1 take 2 requires revision.** The stabilized buckets of 8-12 (low effort) and 10-15 (medium/high effort) are well-calibrated for this persona.
